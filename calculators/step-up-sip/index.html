Understanding the Step-Up SIP

As your income grows over the years, your investments should ideally grow parallel with it. A Step-Up SIP automatically factors in a yearly percentage increase (or 'step up') to your base mutual fund contributions, aggressively compounding your final maturity amount without straining your initial current cashflow.

Core Math: Every 12 months, the Monthly SIP amount is updated to `Current SIP × (1 + StepUp %)` and compounded sequentially.

Common FAQs

Why is a Step-Up SIP better than a normal SIP?

It aligns perfectly with your career lifecycle. As you get annual appraisals and salary hikes, stepping up your SIP manually or automatically utilizes that extra liquidity directly towards long-term wealth, drastically accelerating your time-to-retire while maintaining the same lifestyle proportions.

How much difference does a 10% Step-Up actually make?

Massive. Implementing even a simple 10% annual step-up on a long-term SIP can often functionally DOUBLE your final corpus over 15-20 years compared to remaining completely static.
